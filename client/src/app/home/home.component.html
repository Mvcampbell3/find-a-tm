Welcome to Teammate Finder

good luck and have fun

Looking to find someone to play a game who has a similar skill level as you? Need to find that missing piece to round out the squad? Console players can have a difficult time finding suitable players to play with or against online. Our goal is to provide a place where people can find others who play the same console games they do close to their skill level. You can learn more about our mission here.

To get started, create an account, or log in if you are already signed up. Good Luck!
